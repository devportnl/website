# Cloud Solutions Architect (Azure/AWS)

Are you an experienced architect with a vision for designing scalable, secure, and cost-effective cloud solutions? Do you have deep knowledge of Azure and AWS? Looking for a senior role where you drive impactful transformations? Then we're looking for you!

As a Cloud Solutions Architect at DevPort, you advise clients and design complex cloud infrastructure. You advise on cloud strategy, help with selecting the right services, and design multi-tier architectures that meet current and future client needs. You work with DevOps engineers, security specialists, and development teams to deliver end-to-end solutions.

At DevPort, you're employed by us, but work on secondment for our clients. You get the freedom and rewards of entrepreneurship, with the security of a permanent contract.

## What we're looking for:

* Minimum 7 years of experience in cloud infrastructure, with at least 5 years as an architect
* In-depth knowledge of Azure (AKS, App Service, Azure DevOps, Security Center) AND/OR AWS (EKS, EC2, Lambda, IAM)
* Expertise in cloud security architectures and compliance (SOC2, ISO27001, HIPAA)
* Infrastructure as Code and automation expertise
* Microservices and serverless architecture knowledge
* Cost optimization and cloud financial management
* Networking, storage, and database architecture
* Proven experience with successful cloud migrations and transformations
* Consultancy and communication skills at C-level
* Strong problem-solving skills

## What sets you apart:

* Azure Solutions Architect Expert or AWS Solutions Architect Professional certification (or higher)
* DevOps expertise
* Kubernetes knowledge
* Security architecture background
* Experience with cloud governance frameworks
* Machine Learning / AI platform knowledge

## What we offer:

* You'll be seconded to challenging assignments at our clients - often large enterprise organizations
* Opportunity to drive transformative cloud strategies
* Permanent contract with very attractive employment conditions
* Budget for architect-level certifications (Azure/AWS Solutions Architect)
* Collaboration with senior technical professionals
* Autonomy in architecture decisions and solution design
* Budget for training, conferences, and thought leadership
* Opportunity to contribute to industry knowledge (speaking, publishing)

Are you the Cloud Solutions Architect we're looking for? Send us your CV and let's talk!
